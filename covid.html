Covid Line and Index Chart - Examining Percentages over Time

This chart shows the percentage change of COVID-related data in relation to the Pixstory dataset in the form of a line/index chart.

What is shown here is the 4 different types of COVID-related data biweekly from 11/2020 - 12/2022 for adults 18-24 years old. Users in this age range make up the majority of Pixstory posts in this dataset (55.9%)

  • Red: 7-day average of COVID cases per 100,000 population
  • Blue: Percent of people who had a first dose of a COVID vaccine
  • Green: Percent of people who completed a COVID vaccine primary series (have second dose of a two-dose vaccine or one dose of a single-dose vaccine)
  • Purple: Number of Pixstory posts that mention any COVID vaccine terms

Note: The CDC Dataset does not have any data for the time period of August 1, 2021 - August 15, 2021 thus there is a noticeable trough for all data types in that duration.

  • The CDC dataset only has COVID data beginning Jan 2021 and ending Nov 2022, which influences the data of 7-day average of COVID cases per 100,000 population, percent of people with the first dose, and percent of people who completed the vaccine series.
  • Although the pixstory dataset spans from Jan 12, 2020 to December 8, 2022, users in the 18-24 age range posted from Nov 17, 2020 - December 8, 2022.
