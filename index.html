Pixstory Charts

This page contains documentation for the charts that we have created for Pixstory. The charts are designed to help tell a story behind Pixstory's dataset. It can allow us to examine the interests and priorities of Pixstory users, identify trends in posts' languages, and determine whether the platform is truly "clean".

DSCI 550 (Spring 2023) - Team 5

Language

Language is an important metric to look at for Pixstory; With a multi-line chart, we are able to examine the diverse languages of Pixstory's user base and the change in these language distributions over time - such as examining the growth of a language being used within Pixstory.

Language Metrics

Word Cloud

To create a word cloud chart for Pixstory, we collected data on all words used in the Pixstory dataset, and identified the most commonly used words.

This word cloud shows the top 500 most commonly used words and the larger the word is, the higher frequency it occurs within the datsaet. With this chart, we can glean insights into the concerns and interest of users by identify specific keywords.

Word Cloud Metrics

Hate Words

A tree map that displays a count of all words associated with hate words defined in GLAAD and ADL. Using this chart, oen can determine the prevalence of hate speech on the Pixstory platform. The chart was created by analyzing the text content of each post on the platform and counting the occurrences of specific words associated with hate speech.

The hate words tree map is useful to understand what hate speech is actually spoken on their platform.

This chart will help us determine whether Pixstory truly promotes a safe and clean community.

Hate Words Metrics

Toxicity Speech

A pie chart displaying a count of Detoxify's toxicity scores from 0.1-1.0 in each narrative within the Pixstory posts. The majority of Pixstory's narratives received a toxicity score of <0.1 (97.8%).

It is important to use Detoxify scores, created from a machine learning model, to determine whether Pixstory is a clean social media platform. Combined with the Word Cloud and Hate Words Tree Map, these visualizations show whether Pixstory is truly a positive environment.

Toxicity Speech Metrics

Covid19

An interactive line and index chart that examines Covid-19 trends for Pixstory's users and their engagement with the COVID vaccine topics through their posts by looking at percentage change over time.

The chart shows the frequency of posts related to Covid19, COVID cases over time, and vaccination rates over time.

This interactive Covid19 line/index chart provides insight about the public health efforts during this timeframe of 2020-2022 and whether any correlations exist between Pixstory mentions of COVID vaccines and the number of COVID cases/vaccinations.

Covid19 Metrics

Interests

A Circle Packing Chart that analyzes the top 50 common interests in Pixstory. The chart was generated by analyzing the interests defined by users in their profiles within the interest column.

This chart will allow one to understand the preferences of Pixstory users.
